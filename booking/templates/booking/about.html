About Page

A booking platform built to simplify room reservations.

SpaceFlow helps users discover venues, submit requests clearly, and keeps approvals visible for administrators.

Platform purpose

This platform centralizes room discovery and booking requests for event spaces such as halls, classrooms, conference rooms, studios, and outdoor areas.

How to use it

  • Open the room list and filter spaces by type or capacity.
  • Review the room detail page for venue information and image.
  • Submit the booking form and wait for approval.
  • Administrators manage rooms and booking statuses in Django Admin.
